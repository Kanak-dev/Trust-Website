The Problem

Millions of children in India are in school but not learning.

"Why aren't children going to school?" is no longer our question ,we are asking "What kind of school do they really need?"
needs more helping!

  • 50%

    Of Grade 5 students in India cannot read a grade 2 level text

  • 67%

    Of school pasouts don't enroll in college.

  • 20%

    of Indian youth are considered employable after formal education.

  • 57%

What We Believe

Quality . Skills . Access

We believe every child deserves to learn with joy ,curosity and meaning.

Our solution: Build learning spaces that are:

  • Rooted in foundational literacy & numeracy
  • Powered by real-world, project-based learning
  • Centered on joy, empathy & ownership
  • Accessible to every child, everywhere

Education that doesn't just teach--but transfroms.
